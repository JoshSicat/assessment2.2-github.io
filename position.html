position

Five players are assigned to certain spots on the floor for each team in basketball. These positions have historically been described in terms of the strategic operate they perform on the court. Point guard, shooting guard, small forward, power forward, and center are the five classic positions.

Each of these jobs has certain duties and functions. In the past, players would only play their assigned job, but more recently, players have developed the skills necessary to thrive in a variety of positions and adapt to new roles as needed by the teams they represent.
